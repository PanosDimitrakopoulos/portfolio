Who am I

I am a detail-oriented QA professional specializing in software testing with a growing focus on test automation. With over four years of experience in customer service, I bring a strong user-centric perspective that helps identify edge cases, refine user scenarios, and ensure high-quality product experiences.

My background includes hands-on roles as a QA Tester and Technical Engineer, where I developed a solid foundation in manual testing, bug identification, and cross-functional collaboration. I enjoy working closely with development teams to detect issues early, design effective test cases, and contribute to delivering reliable, user-friendly software.

In recent years, I have developed a strong interest in test automation and have been actively expanding my skills in this area. I have practical experience with Cypress and continuously explore new tools and technologies to improve test coverage, efficiency, and reliability.

I am motivated to grow in a fast-paced, quality-driven environment where I can contribute to building scalable testing solutions while further strengthening my expertise in automation.
